# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project Title
Carbon Emission Impact Analysis & Sustainability Intelligence Platform

---

## 1. Executive Summary
Climate change and rising carbon emissions have become critical global concerns affecting governments, industries, and society. Organizations require data-driven insights to monitor environmental impact, evaluate sustainability performance, and make informed strategic decisions. This project aims to develop an analytics platform that analyzes carbon emissions, renewable energy adoption, energy consumption, and economic indicators to provide meaningful sustainability intelligence.

---

## 2. Business Problem
Decision-makers often lack a centralized analytical platform to understand:

- Which countries contribute most to carbon emissions
- How renewable energy impacts environmental sustainability
- The relationship between economic growth and emissions
- Energy consumption trends over time
- Which regions demonstrate better sustainability performance

Without these insights, policymakers and business leaders may struggle to implement effective sustainability strategies.

---

## 3. Project Objective
The objective of this project is to build an interactive analytics platform that:

- Analyzes carbon emission trends
- Evaluates sustainability indicators
- Measures renewable energy impact
- Studies economic influence on environmental outcomes
- Forecasts future carbon emissions
- Provides business recommendations for sustainability improvement

---

## 4. Stakeholders
The primary stakeholders for this project include:

- ESG Analysts
- Sustainability Managers
- Government Policy Makers
- Energy Sector Analysts
- Environmental Researchers
- Business Strategy Teams
- Corporate Decision Makers

---

## 5. Project Scope

### In Scope
The project includes:

- Multi-source data collection
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Dataset integration and ETL workflow
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- SQL-based business analytics
- KPI engineering
- Interactive dashboard development
- Predictive forecasting
- Business recommendations
- GitHub project documentation

---

### Out of Scope
The project does not include:

- Real-time data streaming
- IoT-based environmental monitoring
- API-based live dashboards
- Satellite data analysis
- Carbon trading simulations

---

## 6. Business Questions
This project aims to answer the following business questions:

1. Which countries have the highest carbon emissions per capita?
2. Does renewable energy adoption reduce carbon emissions?
3. What is the relationship between GDP and carbon emissions?
4. Which countries show strong sustainability performance?
5. How has energy consumption changed over time?
6. What factors most strongly influence carbon emissions?
7. Can future carbon emission trends be predicted?

---

## 7. Functional Requirements

The system should:

- Load environmental and economic datasets
- Merge multiple datasets into a centralized analytical dataset
- Perform business analytical SQL queries
- Generate sustainability KPIs
- Display interactive dashboards
- Allow filtering by country and year
- Show emission trends and comparisons
- Provide forecasting capabilities
- Export filtered analysis results

---

## 8. Non-Functional Requirements

The solution should:

- Be scalable for larger datasets
- Provide responsive dashboard interaction
- Maintain clean modular code structure
- Support reproducibility
- Be easy to deploy and maintain

---

## 9. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The platform will monitor:

- Average CO₂ Emissions Per Capita
- Renewable Energy Percentage
- Average GDP
- Average Energy Consumption
- Carbon Intensity
- Sustainability Score
- Emission Growth Trends

## 14. Risks and Assumptions

### Risks
- Missing data
- inconsistent country naming
- incomplete historical records
- model prediction limitations

### Assumptions
- data sources are reliable
- historical trends are meaningful for forecasting
- sustainability indicators reflect real-world performance
